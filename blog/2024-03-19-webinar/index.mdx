[Isaac Brodsky](https://www.linkedin.com/in/isaacbrodsky/), the CTO of Fused, delved into the power of Fused during a LinkedIn live session with [Matt Forrest](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mbforr/). They discussed the contrast of Python vs. SQL for data analytics, the advantages of serverless geospatial processing, and showcased a live demo of the [UDF Builder](/workbench/udf-builder/).
